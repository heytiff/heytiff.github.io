About this site

Hi, my name is Tiffany and I made this site to serve as my personal digital diary about my life journey. Due to childhood trauma, I have a hard time dealing with people, so I made this site to document what I learn, how I feel, and also to look back later. I never shared this site with anyone, not even friends, not even my long term boyfriend. I love my friends and my boyfriend to death, but this is for me and me only.

What Brought Me Here

At the age of 26, it finally hit me why I have trouble making friends and connecting with people, my childhood trauma that played a major role in my mental development. I decided to not live like that anymore and began the process of healing, starting with accepting the past is the past and I should stop blaming it. The people who did me wrong are still in denial, and as a result, I cut tight with them. So here I am on my journey to become a better person.
